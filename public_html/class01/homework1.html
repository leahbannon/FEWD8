About me

I am a civil servant and product manager at 18F, which works with agencies to build better digital services and websites for the American people. I am also a co-captain of Code for DC, which is a group of volunteers who are passionate about improving local government and the city through technology.

Subscribe to my tech lady newsletter for updates about DC tech events, awesome tech training, women in tech opportunities, the next Tech Lady Hackathon, and sometimes me. Frequency is about 2-3 times per month.
